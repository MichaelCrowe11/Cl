# Sterilization Protocol

## Autoclave Settings
- Temperature: 121°C (250°F)
- Pressure: 15 PSI
- Duration: 15-60 minutes depending on load

## Pre-sterilization Checklist
- [ ] All materials properly wrapped
- [ ] Water level checked
- [ ] Temperature probe calibrated

## Post-sterilization
- [ ] Allow to cool completely
- [ ] Check indicator strips
- [ ] Store in sterile environment
